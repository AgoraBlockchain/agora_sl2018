Sierra Leone Presidential Elections
West District Results

March 7th, 2018

Sierra Leone’s National Electoral Commission introduces Africa’s first ever blockchain elections in collaboration with the Swiss start-up Agora.

Results of the West District were recorded on an unforgeable ledger and are displayed here publicly.

Safe storage of election data and public accessibility is opening a new age for voter confidence and democracy itself in Sierra Leone and in the rest of world.

Agora is a Swiss foundation providing decentralised digital voting systems based on the blockchain technology. Agora’s solution will enable secure and remote voting from digital devices, as well as allow each voter to verify his or her untampered vote in the final count.
